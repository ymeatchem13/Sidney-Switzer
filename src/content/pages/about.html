About Me

Hi, I'm Sidney

and I love Jesus and the nations. My passion for helping people started when my family moved to the Philippines when I was 8. While living there, I saw firsthand the reality of poverty as people lived on a trash dump. It was here that God stirred a desire in me to serve.

Growing up wasn’t always easy—I battled insecurity, depression, and seasons of isolation through middle school, high school and college. But in 2023, at a prayer event with Andre van Zyl, God met me powerfully and affirmed my calling to missions. I graduated in 2024 from the University of Cincinnati and joined a YWAM discipleship training program in Germany. It was here that the Lord deepened my faith and showed me His plans are better than anything I could come up with.

Now, I’m simply a girl chasing after Jesus and helping others discover His love for them.

So now faith, hope, and love abide, these three; but the greatest of these is love.

1 Corinthians 13:33
