About Me

Hi, I'm Sidney!

So good to gave you here.

Thank you for joining me on the journey of a lifetime. God has called me to follow him to the ends of the earth. I don't know what that looks like just yet, but I've said yes!

Join me here as I share my journey of faith, adventures around the world, and the lessons God is teaching me along the way. From jet lag journeys to spiritual revelations, every step is part of His beautiful plan.
